How to use

  1. Log in (one-time). Open Session Setup, pick your OTA (e.g. Agoda), and click "Open Login." Log in manually, then click "Done." Your session cookies are saved so future audits stay logged in.
  2. Set language + currency. Pick a language and the currency auto-updates to match. The tool sets a locale cookie and rewrites URLs so the OTA loads in the correct language and currency. (e.g. ko-kr + KRW for Korean market)
  3. Pick your flow. Choose Flight or Hotel, then fill in search details: airports and departure date for flights, or destination and check-in/check-out for hotels.
  4. Run the audit. Chrome opens automatically, sets the locale, and walks through the full booking flow (search, results, detail, checkout). Takes 1-2 minutes. Do not close the browser window.
  5. Review results. Screenshots appear below. Click to zoom and pan, or download all steps as a zip file.
